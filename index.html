Thoughts on the Progressive Enhancement article

Progressive Enchancement is a strategy that places web content above everything else. Created by Steve Champeon and Nick Finck at SWSX 2003 during their "Inclusive Web Design For the Future‚Äù presentation, Progressive Enchancement eclipses the much older Graceful Degredation model which focuses on the compatibility instead of focusing on the content.

In my opinion, Progressive Enchancement is more important than ever as it focuses on content rather than the system that builds around it. Content is what drives a website and the application should be the one who is adapting around it. Programs evolve at a rapid pace so it will be harder to maintain if you focus on it first rather than the content iself.
